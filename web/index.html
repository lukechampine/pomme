🍎🥔 Pomme

A relay drawing game that lives on Sia decentralized storage.

Start a chain by baking a hot potato, uploading it to your own Sia account, and sending the pass link to a friend. They have 24 hours to pass it on or the chain dies. After 5 hops the potato cools and everyone wins.

Requires a free sia.storage account.
